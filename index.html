ABOUT US

Succeed Youth Association Mutungo is a fully registered community-based organization committed to making education, good health, clean water, and the safety of children a fundamental right. Our association collaborates with organizations, churches, individual volunteers, and entities with shared goals to ensure that children from the most economically disadvantaged families have equal access to education.

Founded with a passion for positive change, Succeed Youth Association operates with a vision to create a world where every child, regardless of socio-economic background, has the opportunity to thrive. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to foster partnerships with schools, communities, and individuals who share our belief in the transformative power of education.

At Succeed Youth Association, we understand that education is not only a key to individual success but also a catalyst for community development. By collaborating with local schools and volunteers, we strive to break down barriers to education, empower vulnerable children, and build a future where every young mind can reach its full potential. Join us on our journey to make a lasting impact on the lives of children and communities we serve.

Location

Succeed Youth Association Mutungo is situated in Bunakijja Village, Katosi Town Council.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in empowering the youth, creating academically competitive individuals, and nurturing socially responsible leaders within Uganda.

Mission

Our mission is to raise a successful and God-fearing generation by providing quality education, promoting skill development, and instilling values that contribute to the holistic growth of every child we serve.

VOLUNTEER WITH US

Succeed Youth Association Mutungo welcomes passionate individuals to join us in making a positive impact on the lives of children in need. As a fully registered community-based organization, we believe in the transformative power of volunteers who are dedicated to contributing to education, good health, and the well-being of children.

Volunteers play a crucial role in our mission, and there are various ways you can get involved:

  • Teaching: Share your knowledge and passion by volunteering to teach children in our community.
  • Counselling: Provide emotional support and guidance to children who may benefit from counseling services.
  • Mentorship Program: Volunteer as a mentor to provide guidance and support to children, helping them develop life skills and make informed decisions about their future.
  • Skill Development Workshops: Organise and conduct workshops that focus on imparting specific skills to children, such as computer literacy, arts and crafts, or vocational skills, to enhance their overall development.
  • Health and Nutrition Initiatives: Volunteer to support health and nutrition programs, conducting awareness sessions on hygiene, organizing health check-ups, or contributing to initiatives that ensure children have access to nutritious meals.
  • Community Outreach and Advocacy: Engage in community outreach activities to raise awareness about the importance of education, child rights, and community development. Advocate for policies that benefit the well-being of children.
  • Fundraising and Resource Mobilisation: Volunteer your time and skills to help with fundraising efforts. This could include organizing events, creating online campaigns, or seeking sponsorships to ensure financial resources for the organization's projects and initiatives.

By volunteering with Succeed Youth Association, you become a vital part of our commitment to ensuring that every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has equal access to education and support.

Join us in creating a positive change in the lives of vulnerable children. Your dedication can make a lasting impact on their futures.
