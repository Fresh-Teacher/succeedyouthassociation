Succeed Youth Association

About Us

Succeed Youth Association Mutungo is a fully registered community-based organization dedicated to making education, good health, clean water, and the safety of children a right and a must. We collaborate with organizations, churches, individual volunteers, and entities with similar goals to ensure that children from the most impoverished families have access to education.

What We Do

Child Sponsorship

We seek kind hearts to support children who have lost their parents and those living with vulnerable guardians.

Clean Water Initiatives

We help communities gain access to clean water by raising funds to build deep water wells.
